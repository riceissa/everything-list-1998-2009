The quantum theory could be an approximation of a smooth universe, or of a
discrete universe with an infinity of quantum levels. We could imagine for
example that at the level of Planck length, time-space is a discrete network
and that there could be an approximative theory which describes it at this
level (like a cellular automata), but that there is a more precize theory
which consider each node of this network as a complex structure (perhaps
discrete or perhaps smooth). Infinity of levels does not necessarily means
nested physical objects which get smaller and smaller, but rather theories
which get more and more precize.

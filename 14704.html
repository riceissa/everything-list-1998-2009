> "ours" is the word you're looking for. A genitive 1st person plural

Furthermore, it's the *independent* form of the 1st person plural
genitive pronoun.

When functioning as a dependent adjective, it's just "our".

Compare

1) the problem is theirs not ours
vs
2) it is their problem not our problem

See my answer to Colin on this point. I assume that you are conscious in much the same
way I am because (roughly speaking) you have a similar brain to mine *and* your
behaviour is similar to mine. If only one of us were conscious we would have to invoke
magic to explain it: God has decided to give only one of us an immaterial, undetectable
soul which does not make any difference to behaviour.
On the other hand, if it turns out that you are an alien robot designed to fool us into
thinking you are human, based on technology utterly different to that in a biological brain,
it is not unreasonable to wonder whether you are conscious at all, or if you are whether
your conscious experience is anything like a human's.

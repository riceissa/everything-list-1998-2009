Now David Deutsch use the comp hypothesis explicitly, and, unless I have
been wrong comp entails that the laws of physics originates from what the
consistent (immaterial) machines/programs/relative-numbers are able
to anticipate correctly.

There is no magic there, just "Theoretical Artificial Intelligence" +
some amount of arithmetical platonism. (Very well defended by David also).

Mind object are just object like the game of Bridge, numbers, programs,
software, and plausibly quarks and gluons too ...

them are deducible from a deeper theory which is just number theory +
inside views captured by the logic of incompleteness phenomena and variants.

origin of empirical laws. This is not obvious!

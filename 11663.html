Ermm yes. But note that Bruno is claiming that
(the appearance of) matter arises from "Platonic" (my
and Plato's usage) existence. Whereas I am claiming
that (the concept of) numbers emerges
from matter (mathematicians).

So it is not just a case of where you are in the hierarchy,
it is case of which way up it is.

This would be unheard of, George. I communicated over
the past 2 decades with more than a dozen discussion

unimaginable way to mark the topic one is writing
about.

wrote in a subject that it will mess up the archive
search for not tracing back the post to its origins.)
But 'everything' has at least two sides.

Thanks for introducing such revolutionary ideas!

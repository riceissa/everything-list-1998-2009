If you admit non-local hidden variables then you can have a theory like Bohmian quantum mechanics in which randomness is all epistemological, like statistical mechanics, and there is no place for multiple-worlds.

I'm not sure what you mean by "locally". Since they have opposite charge they will be attracted by photon exchanges and will fall into some hydrogen atom state by emission of photons.

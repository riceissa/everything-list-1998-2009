Visit http://www.geocities.com/ResearchTriangle/9356/WebAward1.htm to
complete a short, simple application for a new monthly web site award
dedicated exclusively to the research, development and education of
futurist, transhumanist, extropian and related disciplines. This is not
simply a web design award but also has an emphasis on content and
contribution to these allied fields related to futurism, transhumanism
and extropy for the study and improvement of humankind. A unique link
back to this site with a customized citation describing each winning
site's contribution to this nascent area of activity will be provided at
a comprehensive winner's list maintained at the host web site, The
Futurist Think Tank. Feel free to apply for your own site or to
nominate another site for inclusion in this review. come back later to
view some excellent work in these new disciplines of human endeavor.

The Ross Model

I have proposed what I believe is the simplest yet model of the
universe. I call my model the “The Ross Model of the Universe”. The
Ross Model can be used to describe and explain all elements of the
universe including photons, electrons, protons, neutrons, atomic nuclei,
heat, temperature, magnetism and gravity. According to this model, the
entire universe and everything in it from photons to electrons to
protons to galaxies is comprised of nothing but a single type of
elementary charged point particle. I call these point particles,
pointicles or (more often) “tronnies”. To the best of my knowledge no
one in the history of the world ever suspected that tronnies existed. I
have identified them and I believe that they must be the basic building
blocks of the universe. A tronnie has no mass and no volume but it has
a charge equal to + e or – e.

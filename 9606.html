Right, except I think thoughtful scientists do know it. They recognize that what we call
"objective" would be more accurately called "intersubjective agreement". We create models and when
they work for everybody we (tentatively) agree on them. A lot of physics now is derived from
symmetry principles and the most basic principle is invariance of the model under changing
subjective viewpoints.

I point out that there may already be something of a precedent for my
Everything.

We consider numbers as just points, but numbers contain numbers. Points
within points.

We recognize different points within a point combinations but give them no
corresponding structural distinction - they remain just a point.

My Everything is similar but at a higher intermixing - shifting points
within points within points within.... but never the less still just a
point - flux without structural change.

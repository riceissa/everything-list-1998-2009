An information scientist at another forum told me that the infinity of the hyperreal numbers is larger than that of the reals, & that the infinity of the surreals is larger than that of the hyperreals. Is this true? And does the infinity of curves or functions (of the standard or "archimedean" numbers) correspond to either the infinity of the hyperreals or that of the surreals? Is it known whether one could possibly define a larger set of numbers than the surreals?

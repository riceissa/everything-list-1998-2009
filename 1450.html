Climbing the ladder is unimportant - enumerating the possibiltiies
is. There is no difficulty in enumerating a tree, and the two
different results obtained from assuming either of the set theory
axioms you mentioned simply result in different branches. Both will be
enumerated by Schmidhubers Great Programmer, so both will get an
appropriate amouynt of measure.

After many years of investigation not only have I come to the
conclusion that the Universe is a totally materialistic process but
that the present state of physics is that of an abstractionist
paradigm which can not express that totally materialistic
process.

Also, during my investigation a materialist paradigm evolved and
takes the form of an hierarchical structure of numbers and
arrows which can be viewed at
<http://members.xoom.com/_paradigm>.

The materialist paradigm is the paradigm of science and pertains
to the formation, functioning, and evolution of everything.

At present I'm pursing its application to the structure and
functioning of the brain. The two sides of the paradigm
represent the two hemispheres of the brain.

The bottom line is that mathematics is not the ultimate language
and logic of the Universe, and the present state of physics is that
of an abstractionist paradigm which is overthrown by the
materialist paradigm.
